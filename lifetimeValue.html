MEASURE LIFETIME VALUE

Get a high-resolution view of what each customer contributes to your business

Data analytics allows you to quantify what each customer contributes to you over time, and can be adapted to how your business works. For an advertising-based content-marketing business, Data Poetry applied a framework of micro-level and macro-level lifetimes: looking at the sequence of value-positive and value-negative events for individual users as they navigating the business's website over one or more visits, and the change over weeks and months in the content and products that were relevant to them.
